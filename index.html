About Us

Federal Contracting and Subcontracting

AI Solutions 87 LLC is a service disabled veteran owned small business (SDVOSB) that provides experts in computational data science, high performance computing, simulation optimization, and IoT devices for remote sensing, data collection, and computing at the edge. Our team is ready to help you create, improve, or deploy custom applications on your supercomputers, up in the cloud, or at the tactical edge.

DUNS, CAGE Code, and NAICS Codes

AIS is a registered SDVOSB in SAM, and you can find us by looking up our UEI: RXFLA65SEJL9  or CAGE Code: 8DQH0. Also, our experts have active U.S. Security Clearances in JPAS, so we can even help you in your SCIFs!
